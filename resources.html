Resources

When a loved one dies by Suicide, many people find it helpful and healing to join a bereavement/support group and meet others who really understand their emotional pain. All Survivors of Suicide Loss are Welcome to attend our FREE monthly meetings. Participants receive emotional strength, education, resources, learn coping strategies, AND know that they are not alone!!
