Bringing Depression and Suicide Prevention into the Spotlight!

Our 2019 Scholarships Awards Winners are: Corina and Jason from Bridgewater/Raritan High School

It is with great pleasure that we continue to honor High School Graduates who are continuing their education in Psychology, who posess outstanding academic achievements and accomplishments in community service.


Some thank you notes from our recipients...

”I am writing to extend my personal thanks for selecting me as one of this year recipients of the Joao Jose ‘John’ Santos Memorial Scholarships Awards. The very generous scholarship which your organization funded will go a long way to make my college expenses easier to manage. This organization is very inspiring to me and my fellow peers for all that it stands for and in memory of John Santos” --jk


‘I am sincerely honored to have been selected as a recipient of the Joao Jose ‘John’ Santos Memorial Scholarship. Each year, the cost of college tuition rises, so I highly value any monetary help I receive. Thank you for your generosity which allows me to explode my college opportunities with less burden. In fact, I plan to volunteer at the Medical Center or MD Cancer Hospital to gain experience in a hospital setting but also to interact with people with different past experiences and conditions. Upon receipt of your organization, I am also motivated to continue practice compassion and understanding towards all, especially those facing mental health challenges. Thank you again for your thoughtful gift. I cherish it deeply and know that it positions me one step closer to achieving my goals. I hope one day I can help students the same way that your organization has helped me!” --cd


“I cannot thank you enough for selecting me as one of the recipients of the Joao Jose ‘John’ Santos Memorial Scholarships. It is truly an honor to be selected to represent your organization! As you know, I intend to study neuroscience in college in the hopes of researching the effects of concussions on suicide. Thank you again for a true honor!!” --jl


“Thank you very much for honoring me as a recipient of the Joao Jose ‘John’ Santos Memorial Scholarship. This cause is very dear to my heart and I appreciate that recognized me as a deserving of this award. I plan to put it toward my college expenses” --nk
